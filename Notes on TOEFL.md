## Integrated Essay

**The Basics**
1. 3 mins to read about an academic topic
	1. 250-300 words
2. 2 mins to listen to the lecture on the same topic
3. 20 mins to write an essay using details from both
---
**The relationship**
1. article argument and lecture challenges it
	1. *most common*
2. article describes a problem and lecture gives solution to the problem
3. article describes solutions and lecture challenges them.
*opposing arguments from the article and lecture*

---
**Article**
1. main idea
2. then 3 supporting arguments
**Lecture**
3. its own main point
4. then 3 supporting arguments
	1. *corresponding to the arguments in the article*
	2. and in the same *order*
*include as much details as possible*

---
**notes taking**

Again, R for reading and L for lecture
- do make your correspondence using arrows or sth like that

---
**Template**
1. The reading and the lecture are both about...
2. While the author of the reading feels... the lecturer challenges the author's ideas.
3. He does not believe that...

4. First, the author notes that...
5. according to the article...
6. The professor, though, challenges this idea.
7. He claims that...
8. Moreover, he mentions that...

9. next, the author argues that...
10. The professor is not convinced by this claim.
11. He mentions that...

12. Finally, the author points out that...
13. on the other hand, the professor says that...

## 二、高级句式 (通用模板与示例)

### 引出观点 (Introduction/Topic Sentence)

- **模板:** It is widely acknowledged that [观点], which *underscores* the importance of [主题].
- **示例:** It is widely acknowledged that education fosters critical thinking, which underscores the importance of a holistic curriculum.
    
- **模板:** While some may argue that [对立观点], it is more *compelling* to *assert* that [你的观点].
- **示例:** While some may argue that technology isolates individuals, it is more compelling to assert that it enhances global connectivity.

### 因果关系 (Cause and Effect)

- **模板:** The *proliferation* of [现象] has *precipitated* [结果], *thereby* [进一步影响].
- **示例:** The proliferation of automation has precipitated job displacement, thereby necessitating reskilling programs.
	- 自动化技术的普及导致了工作岗位的流失，因此需要开展技能再培训项目。

- **模板:** *Owing* to [原因], [现象] has become increasingly *prevalent*, which in turn [结果].
- **示例:** Owing to rapid urbanization, environmental degradation has become increasingly prevalent, which in turn threatens biodiversity.
	- 由于城市化进程加快，环境恶化现象日益普遍，进而威胁生物多样性。

### 举例说明 (Examples)

- **模板:** A *quintessential* example of [观点/现象] is [具体例子], which *exemplifies* [进一步解释].
    
- **示例:** A quintessential example of technological innovation is renewable energy, which exemplifies sustainable development.
	- 可再生能源是技术创新的典型例子，也是可持续发展的典范。
    
- **模板:** This can be illustrated by [例子], wherein [具体细节], thereby demonstrating [观点].
- **示例:** This can be illustrated by online learning platforms, wherein students gain autonomy, thereby demonstrating the potential of technology in education.
	- 在线学习平台可以很好地说明这一点，学生在其中获得了自主权，从而展现了技术在教育领域的潜力。
    

### 让步与反驳 (Concession and Rebuttal)

- **模板:** *Admittedly*, [对立观点] may hold true in certain contexts; however, [你的观点] is more *substantiated* due to [理由].
- **示例:** Admittedly, social media may distract students; however, its role in fostering global awareness is more substantiated due to enhanced connectivity.
	- 诚然，社交媒体可能会分散学生的注意力；然而，由于网络连接的增强，它在培养全球意识方面的作用也得到了更充分的证实。
    
- **模板:** Although [对立观点], the *overriding* significance of [你的观点] lies in [理由].
- **示例:** Although traditional teaching methods ensure discipline, the overriding significance of innovative pedagogy lies in cultivating creativity.
	- 虽然传统的教学方法能够确保纪律，但创新教学法的首要意义在于培养创造力。
    

### 结论 (Conclusion)

- **模板:** *In light of the foregoing*, it is *unequivocal* that [重申观点], which *warrants* [建议/展望].
- **示例:** In light of the foregoing, it is unequivocal that environmental conservation is paramount, which warrants global cooperation.
	- 鉴于上述情况，环境保护至关重要，这一点毋庸置疑，因此需要全球合作。
    
- **模板:** Ultimately, [重申观点] not only [直接影响] but also [长远影响].
- **示例:** Ultimately, fostering resilience not only enhances personal growth but also contributes to societal progress.

## 三、使用建议

**1. 词汇使用:**

- 避免堆砌高级词汇，确保词汇与语境匹配。
- 每个段落使用 2-3 个高级词汇，替换常见表达，保持自然。
- 注意词汇的固定搭配，如 "foster creativity" (正确) 而非 "foster idea" (不自然)。

**2. 句式运用:**

- 每篇文章作文至少使用 1-2 种高级句式 (如让步句、因果句)，增强逻辑性和多样性。
- 将句式模板套用到不同话题，练习改写。例如，将因果句模板应用于教育、科技等话题。
- 确保句式服务于论证逻辑，避免为了用高级句式而牺牲内容清晰度。

**3. 练习方法:**

- 针对托福常见话题 (如教育、科技、环境)，用上述词汇和句式写 150-200 字的段落，模拟考试场景。
- 检查高级词汇和句式的语法准确性，如主谓一致、时态正确性及搭配合理性 (如 "foster" 常接抽象名词，如 "foster creativity")。
